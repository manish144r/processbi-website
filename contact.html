Common Questions

FAQ

Do you work with organisations outside Victoria?
Yes — projects have spanned NSW, Victoria, Queensland, South Australia, and Western Australia. Travel is standard for on-site workshops and project kickoffs; most ongoing delivery is remote. Fully Australian-based, AEST/AEDT time zone throughout.
How quickly can you start a project?
Current availability varies. For advisory work, we can usually schedule within a week. For projects, typical lead time is 2–4 weeks for scoping and contracting. Urgent engagements are sometimes possible — ask.
Do you work with subcontractors, or is it just the principal?
Primarily the principal architect. For large projects, trusted specialist partners are engaged for specific domains — deep SAP BW integration, enterprise network security, actuarial analytics — but the principal remains the design authority and accountable deliverer throughout. You are never getting a bait-and-switch: the person you speak to is the person who builds it.
We're locked into our current vendor. Can you still help?
Usually yes. Most engagements involve working within existing vendor contracts rather than ripping them out. The question is always: what's the best outcome from where you are now, not what a blank-slate redesign would look like.
We're a government agency with strict procurement rules. Can you work with us?
Yes — ProcessBI has delivered for NSW Government agencies across emergency services, metropolitan rail, and public administration. Familiar with ICT procurement panels, NSW Government supplier requirements, Digital Transformation Agency (DTA) standards, and standard government contract frameworks including SOW and MSA arrangements.
